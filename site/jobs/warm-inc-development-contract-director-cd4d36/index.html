About This Role

JOB DESCRIPTION

DIRECTOR PLANNING, DEVELOPMENT, AND CONTRACT MANAGEMENT

We all Really Matter (WARM) is the leading grassroots, minority-led CBO 501 ( c ) 3 provider of comprehensive services for victims of domestic violence in greater Harlem and the South Bronx. WARM’s mission is to prevent and interrupt the cycle of abuse for women and communities of color.

Reporting directly to the Executive Director, with wide latitude for independent judgment, the Director of Planning, Development, and Contract Management will serve as a member of the Executive Management Team. The Director will be the principal creator of and eventual liaison to the Development Committee of the Board of Directors and key organization members, as WARM continues to rapidly grow its services and geographic reach.

Key Responsibilities

Fundraising

● Drive revenue generating activities to meet the budget of existing, expanded, and new programs

● Identify and secure funds from three levels of government and foundations/corporations ● Develop proposals for current programs and new funding opportunities ● Supervise staff as WARM hires development consultants and assistants ● Develop and implement major donor campaign; work with board, executive staff, and

consultants to identify, increase, and manage a portfolio of donors; maintain ongoing relationships with major donors

● Oversee organization of special events, with the goal of increasing net revenue ● Manage the implementation of Raiser’s Edge and oversee staff responsible for data entry and gift processing

● Maintain and monitor monthly chart of proposals and reports due and ensure products are of the best quality and submitted on time

● Generate donor communications, newsletters, and annual fundraising reports ● Assist in creation of print, digital, and social media content in support of fundraising

Planning

● Develop and execute WARM’s annual fundraising plan to meet expense budgets for programs, capital, infrastructure, and fund balance needs

● Create and execute a strategy for increased general support revenue through individual donors, special events, foundations/corporations, and new revenue sources ● Coordinate development planning with program directors/supervisors for new and existing programs

Contract Management

● Monitor all government, foundation, and corporate contracts to meet goals, objectives, outcomes, and deliverables

● Draft contracts and/or oversee contract development for funded government proposals ● Develop quality assurance capacity with CEO, formulate corrective action plans and monitor results

● Oversee contract management system, including progress reports to funders and maintenance of a contract management database

● Review and approve justifications for contract budget and program modifications ● Prepare and present regular reports to CEO/board on progress towards fundraising goals

Qualifications/Competencies

● Passion for and identification with agency mission

● BA (MA or equivalent preferred) and seven plus years’ experience as a professional fundraiser for sizable non-profit organizations, including five years in a leadership role ● Demonstrated success scaling funding for a non-profit organization

● Proven record of attracting and securing significant grants form government, corporations and foundations

● Demonstrated ability to raise funds through major donors and special events ● Strong financial management skills, including budgeting

● Experience with database systems and procedures of donor tracking and reporting ● Microsoft Office and spreadsheet proficiency

● Experience with online fundraising, email marketing, and internet research ● High energy, proactive, results-oriented; able to set and surpass goals; ability to influence and persuade key stakeholders; comfortable working with a high degree of autonomy and responsibly; exceptional ability to work with teams; well-organized with close attention to detail and follow-through; strong executive presence; strategic, creative, progressive thinker; strong interpersonal, verbal communication and presentation skills
