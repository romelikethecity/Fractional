About Fractional Pulse

The leading job board and market intelligence platform for fractional executives.

Our Mission

Fractional Pulse exists to connect experienced executives with companies that need their expertise on a part-time or project basis. We believe the future of executive talent is flexible, and we're building the infrastructure to make that future a reality.

What We Do

We aggregate fractional executive opportunities from across the market, providing:

  • Curated Job Listings - Fractional CFO, CMO, CTO, COO, and other C-suite roles
  • Salary Intelligence - Market rate data for fractional executive compensation
  • Company Profiles - Insights into companies hiring fractional talent
  • Market Trends - Analysis of the fractional executive landscape

Who We Serve

Fractional Pulse serves two audiences:

  • Experienced Executives looking for fractional, part-time, or interim opportunities that leverage their expertise across multiple companies
  • Growing Companies seeking senior leadership without the commitment of a full-time hire

The Fractional Movement

The fractional executive model is transforming how companies access senior talent. Startups, scale-ups, and established businesses are increasingly turning to fractional leaders who bring:

  • Deep expertise without full-time cost
  • Flexibility to scale engagement up or down
  • Fresh perspectives from working across industries
  • Immediate impact without lengthy onboarding
